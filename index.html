To bring the old school Wasteland experience to ARMA III, we run a set of high quality mods developed by some of the best community modders. Such as:

AiA Terrain Pack

AiA Terrain Pack provides classic terrains from ARMA II such as Chernarus, Takistan, Utes, Saharani plus many more. Take a look at some of the screenshots below, or head over to the official BI Forums thread here for more information.

Massi Weapons

The Massi Weapons pack provides a cache of your favourite weapons from ARMA II, such as the M4A1/M16, HK416/417, G36, SCAR, G3, FN FAL, M249, MK48, M107 and many many more. Check out the images below, or head over to the official BI Forums thread here to see the full run down.

Massi Vehicles

Much like the weapons pack from Massi, this vehicle pack provides a plethora of vehicles upgraded from ARMA II & ready to roll in ARMA III with working FFV (Fire from Vehicles) support. Take a look at the images below or click here to head over to the official BI Forums thread.
